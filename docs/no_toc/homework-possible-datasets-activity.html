Course Name

4.4 Homework: Possible Datasets Activity

4.4.1 Activity

Estimated time: 50 min

4.4.1.1 Instructions

  1. Skim three abstracts

  2. Pick one and answer the following questions

  1. Notice – What about this abstract most interests you?

  2. Dataset – Summarize at a high level where the samples came from, how many there are, and what technology was used for sequencing.

  3. Wonder – Two or three questions you would like to ask using this (and any other) datasets.

  1. Post your answers by replying to the “Project Work: Possible Datasets” topic in the Discussion Forum
